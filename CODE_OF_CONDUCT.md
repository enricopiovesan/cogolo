# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Cogolo aims to be a respectful, constructive, and technically rigorous open-source project.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent while being honest about technical concerns
- focus feedback on the work, not the person
- welcome questions from contributors at different experience levels
- keep discussions aligned with the project’s standards and goals

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- sustained hostility
- bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code of conduct.
